Goal:
- Ship an MVP static web app where a user submits a query and sees:
  1) "Gemma Overview" generated locally in-browser (query-only input),
  2) Google Programmable Search Engine (PSE) results underneath.
- No accounts, no persistence, no server-side query/output storage.
